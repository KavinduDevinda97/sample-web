The World of Mobizz

Over 10 years old, Mobizz is a growing company of diverse group of businesses. Mobizz human resource are rapidly growing service providers in the recruitment sector and human resource management, and our operations are across the Asian regions. In line of claim management, we collaborated with leading international insurance company and provide the travel claim management services covering Europe. And excelling in corporate claim and Outpatient Department management in Sri Lanka.
